Welcome

LetsTagOn is a networking and collaboration platform for change champions to connect with organizations and other volunteers for a cause.

LetsTagOn provides volunteer opportunities, enables volunteer connection, and aims to help them “Tag On” to improve the collective efforts.

The platform with further features is under development. But that doesn't stop us from on-boarding you! Featured volunteering events are on home page. You can register to those based on your interest and availability. We are also working with corporate and educational institutes volunteers on some interesting volunteering projects. Get in touch with us If your team wants to be part of them.

True to its vision, the platform is being developed by a set of volunteers who are passionate about technology solutions for this sector. If you want to get updates and want access to a beta version or just want to say hello, do write to us!

How It Works

Volunteers

  • Share your story and update contributions to inspire more people to volunteer
  • Track and share history of your contributions which would help you build a volunteering profile
  • Connect and network with people having similar interests
  • Look for events to participate and opportunities to contribute

NGOs/Lead Volunteers

  • Increase reach of your causes to a larger set of individuals who are interested to contribute
  • Reduce cost of volunteer acquisition
  • Build credibility through recommendation and network influence
  • Track, maintain and share history of your activities

Corporates

  • Nurture kindness in your employees and provide opportunities to contribute to social causes
  • Consolidate and align the individual contribution of employees towards CSR efforts

Educational Institutes

  • Make your community service effective and visible
  • Leverage your large student volunteer base for greater collective outcomes
  • Build student all-round personality through volunteering
