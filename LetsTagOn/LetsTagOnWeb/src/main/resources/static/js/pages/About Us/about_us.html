About Us

LetsTagOn is a networking and collaboration platform for change champions to connect with a cause, organizations and other volunteers. It provides volunteer opportunities, enables volunteer connection and aims to help them Tag On to improve the collective efforts in bringing in the desired social impact. LetsTagOn offers the following benefits for change enablers.

Volunteers
  • Share your story and update contributions to inspire more people to volunteer
  • Track and share history of your contributions which would help you build a volunteering profile
  • Connect and network with people and organizations having similar interests
  • Look for events to participate and opportunities to contribute NGOs/ Lead volunteers
NGOs/ Lead volunteers

  • Increase reach of your causes to a larger set of individuals who are interested to contribute
  • Build credibility through recommendation and network influence
  • Track, maintain and share history of your activities Corporates
Corporates
  • Nurture kindness in your employees and provide opportunities to contribute to social causes
  • Consolidate and align the individual contribution of employees towards CSR efforts Educational institutes
Educational institutes
  • Make your community service effective and visible
  • Leverage your large student volunteer base for greater collective outcomes
  • Build student all-round personality through volunteering
